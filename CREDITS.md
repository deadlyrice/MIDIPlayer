# Credits & Asset Provenance

This document records the licensing/provenance of the bundled audio assets, to
keep the app clear of third-party copyright (App Store Review Guideline 5.2).

## SoundFont

- **GeneralUser GS** v2.0.x — by S. Christian Collins.
- Source: https://github.com/mrbumpy409/GeneralUser-GS (and https://schristiancollins.com)
- License: see `MIDIPlayer/sound bank/GeneralUser-GS-LICENSE.txt`. Permits use in
  software projects, including commercial use, with no attribution required. The
  bundled samples allow full use in music production for profit.

## Sample MIDI files (`MIDIPlayer/samples/`)

All sample MIDIs are original transcriptions, generated programmatically, of
melodies whose musical copyright has long expired (public domain). They contain
no third-party arrangement, recording, or sequencing data.

| File | Melody | Composer / origin | Status |
|------|--------|-------------------|--------|
| `Ode to Joy.mid` | "Ode to Joy" | Beethoven, Symphony No. 9 (1824) | Public domain |
| `Twinkle Twinkle.mid` | "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" | "Ah! vous dirai-je, maman" (18th c.) | Public domain |
| `Mary Had a Little Lamb.mid` | "Mary Had a Little Lamb" | Traditional | Public domain |
| `Fur Elise.mid` | "Für Elise" (opening) | Beethoven (1810) | Public domain |

These replaced earlier sample files (copyrighted popular songs) and a SoundFont
derived from the Creative/E-mu sample ROM, both of which were unsuitable for
distribution.
